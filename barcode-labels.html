Barcode & Scanner Label Solutions

High-Quality Barcode Labels for All Industries

Barcode labels are essential for modern inventory management, retail operations, logistics, and manufacturing. At Vertexx Label Printing, we specialize in self-adhesive barcode labels compatible with all major scanners, including laser, handheld, and mobile devices.

Our barcode labels are manufactured to ensure high print resolution, durability, and scan accuracy. They can withstand wear and tear, moisture, temperature changes, and chemical exposure depending on the application. Each label is carefully designed to meet your product and operational requirements.

From retail packaging and product identification to shipping labels and warehouse inventory, our barcode labels are suitable for multiple industries including FMCG, healthcare, electronics, and logistics. We also offer customized sizes, formats, and materials to integrate seamlessly with your operations.

Key Features of Our Barcode Labels
  • High-resolution printing for accurate scanning
  • Durable adhesive suitable for multiple surfaces
  • Compatible with all types of barcode scanners
  • Resistant to moisture, heat, and chemicals
  • Custom sizes, shapes, and materials available

Printing Options

Lamination

Clear protective film applied to enhance durability, moisture resistance, tear resistance and overall visual appeal.

Varnishing

Transparent coating to protect against abrasion, sunlight, chemicals and moisture while adding a glossy finish.

Serial Numbering

Unique numbering system for identification, retail, logistics and inventory tracking.

Hot / Cold Foil

Metallic foil stamping for premium branding. Available in gold, metallic and various custom finishes.

Embossing

Raised design printing that enhances appearance and discourages counterfeiting.

Bar-Coding

Machine-readable barcodes for secure product identification and validation.

Holograms

Anti-counterfeit security with unique optical properties and stylish finish.

Screen Printing

Security / Specialty Ink
