Pressure Sensitive Labels

Vertexx Label Printing provides high-quality pressure sensitive labels designed to adhere instantly to surfaces without heat or water. These labels offer convenience, reliability, and versatility for a wide range of applications.

Why Choose Vertexx Pressure Sensitive Labels?

Our labels stick securely on various surfaces instantly, ensuring durability and ease of application while maintaining print clarity.

  • Instant adhesion without heat or moisture
  • Durable materials for long-lasting labeling
  • Custom sizes, shapes, and adhesives
Reliable labeling solution for industries requiring fast and secure adhesion.

Specialization:

We specialize in pressure sensitive labels for retail, logistics, pharmaceuticals, manufacturing, and industrial applications. Vertexx ensures precise die-cutting, adhesive consistency, and compatibility with thermal, laser, and inkjet printing.

Key Features:

Engineered for quick application and long-lasting adhesion.

  • Wide range of adhesive types (permanent, removable, freezer-grade)
  • Smooth surface for thermal, inkjet, and laser printing
  • Custom shapes, sizes, and colors

Applications:

Ideal for industries and uses that demand reliable, ready-to-stick labeling.

  • Packaging, shipping, and logistics
  • Retail product identification and branding
  • Pharmaceuticals, healthcare, and manufacturing
